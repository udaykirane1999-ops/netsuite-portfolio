# OTC / P2P / R2R NetSuite Implementation

## Business Context
A multi-subsidiary organization was using NetSuite to manage sales, procurement, accounting, and inventory across multiple business units. The goal was to standardize Order-to-Cash, Procure-to-Pay, and Record-to-Report processes while maintaining strong financial controls and reporting.

## My Role
I worked as a NetSuite Functional Consultant responsible for gathering business requirements, configuring NetSuite, supporting integrations, and coordinating testing and deployment activities.

## Key Work Performed
- Led discovery sessions with Sales, Finance, and Operations teams  
- Configured roles, permissions, and approval workflows  
- Set up sales orders, purchase orders, invoices, and vendor bills  
- Configured posting rules and accounting preferences  
- Built Saved Searches and dashboards for daily business tracking  
- Supported month-end close and financial reporting  

## Tools & Features Used
NetSuite ERP, SuiteFlow, Saved Searches, SuiteAnalytics, Roles & Permissions, Jira

## Outcome
The implementation improved transaction accuracy, reduced manual processing, and provided better visibility into revenue, inventory, and financial performance.
