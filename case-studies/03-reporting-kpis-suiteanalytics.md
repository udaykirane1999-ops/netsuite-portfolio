# NetSuite Reporting, KPIs & SuiteAnalytics

## Business Context
Business users required real-time visibility into sales, procurement, inventory, and financial performance.

## My Role
I designed and built Saved Searches, KPIs, and dashboards for different user roles across Sales, Finance, and Operations.

## Key Responsibilities
- Created AR aging, revenue, backlog, and inventory searches
- Built KPI scorecards for executives and finance teams
- Designed SuiteAnalytics dashboards for operational tracking
- Delivered reports for month-end close and management reviews

## Tools & Features
Saved Searches, SuiteAnalytics, KPIs, Dashboards, NetSuite Reporting

## Outcome
Users gained real-time insights into business performance, enabling faster decision-making and better financial control.
